# About Crutchless

I created Crutchless in an attempt to document some of the Joys and Struggles of tyring to live an entertainment free life. By entertainment I mean using any application to consume content optimized to maintain user attention to sell adspace or video games that are also designed to maintain user attention. That definition is oddly specific but it neatly represents the habits that I am trying to break. No more games, no more Youtube, no more Facebook, no scrolling, no memes, no using my crutch to escape. Why?

## Remember the gods you Worshipped and how they treated you

In April of 2022 I was preaching to the people of the Dominican Republic, walking down some dusty road when those words pierced my soul like a dagger. Hundreds of miles away from home, my heart longed for the solice of playing a video game. And God told me to remember. He always does. I remember spending hours building a Minecraft castle instead of completing homework. I remember staring at a phone screen till 3am watching Youtube instead of getting a fresh start to the next day. I remember those souless eyes staring back at me from a dark loading screen in a basement waiting for a game to load. The gods of entertainment made a slave of me and I remember how it felt.

In part I write this blog to remember. In part I write this blog to share my journey with others who experience the same struggles and loss that I have. In part I write this blog because I know I can so I might as well document the process and have others hold me accountable.
